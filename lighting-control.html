Lighting Control Systems

Manage your house from near or far. Whether you are on the couch or on vacation, a lighting control system gives you complete command of every light in your home from your phone, tablet, or dedicated keypad.

Turn lights on when you are away to make it look like someone is home. Set schedules to automatically adjust lighting throughout the day. Save electricity by ensuring lights are only on when you need them. It is convenience, security, and savings all in one system.

Why Automate

Benefits of Lighting Control

Lighting automation is one of the smartest upgrades you can make to your home. Here is why.

Convenience

Control every light in your home from one app or keypad. Set scenes with a single press — movie night, dinner party, or goodnight.

Security

Simulate occupancy while you are away. Schedule lights to turn on and off at random intervals so it always looks like someone is home.

Energy Savings

Automated schedules and occupancy sensors ensure lights are only on when needed. Dimming extends bulb life and reduces your electric bill.

Scene Control

Create custom lighting scenes for any occasion. One press sets the perfect mood — bright for cooking, dim for movies, warm for entertaining.
