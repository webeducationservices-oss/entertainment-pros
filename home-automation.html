Smart Home Solutions

Home Automation | Design, Installation, and Training

Your personal preferences determine what home automation system you should install. What apps do you like to use? Do you prefer voice commands? Which platform is most secure? Which is easiest for your family to use? These are the questions we help you answer before a single device gets installed.

The smart home market is flooded with options — smart lightbulbs, smart TVs, automated shades, voice-activated outlets, smart thermostats, and more. But what do you really need? The answer depends entirely on how you live. Some homeowners want a simple voice-controlled lighting system. Others want a fully integrated smart home where climate, security, entertainment, and lighting work together seamlessly.

At Entertainment Pros, we cut through the noise. We listen to how you use your home, then design an automation system that fits your lifestyle — not the other way around. And we don’t just install it and leave. We train you and your family so everyone is comfortable using the system from day one.

Why Automate

Advantages of Home Automation

A well-designed smart home is not a luxury — it’s a practical upgrade that saves time, energy, and money.

Easier Home Management

Control lights, climate, entertainment, and security from a single app or voice command. Simplify your daily routine.

Added Security

Smart locks, cameras, and motion sensors add a layer of security. Get alerts on your phone when something needs attention.

Energy Savings

Schedule your climate and lighting systems so they run only when needed. Automated adjustments mean lower utility bills every month.

Peace of Mind

Never worry about leaving the stove on, the garage door open, or the lights running. Smart alerts and auto-shutoff give you confidence.
