Outdoor Living, Elevated: Creating the Perfect Pool & Backyard Sound System

May 10, 2023 | By Entertainment Pros

The Secrets to the Ultimate Home Theater Experience

Note: this Q & A session was inspired by a clients comment to us...

"When we go to my neighbors' homes, the music by their pools sucks. Can you install a sound system that doesn't suck?"

- YES WE CAN, and here is how we do it!

Enjoying the year-round outdoor experience is the essence of Florida living. With so much time spent outside, it's only fair you get to appreciate every moment.

That's why we created this Q&A guide. We want you to know what it takes to design a great backyard sound system, things to steer clear of when researching, and how to set priorities when planning your sound system.

Whether you're looking to create a relaxing oasis or a party atmosphere, understanding these key factors will help you make informed decisions and design a system that meets your unique needs. So, let's dive into the world of pool and backyard sound systems and learn how to create the perfect outdoor audio experience.


Q: What makes a great Pool Sound System or Backyard Sound System?


A: A great pool or backyard sound system is all about quality sound and speaker placement. Quality sound comes from having enough power to compete with the outside noise, considering the space, square footage, driver size, number of speakers, and having high-quality equipment.

The speaker placement is also crucial to ensure even sound distribution throughout the pool area and create an immersive experience for you and your guests. Aesthetics matter; you need to place your speakers strategically to get the best sound quality.


Q: What happens if I move my indoor speakers outside? What if I use the wrong equipment outside?


A: Moving indoor speakers outside can result in the deterioration of the speaker's structure, wires, and equipment due to exposure to humidity, weather, and other environmental factors. Indoor speakers are not designed to withstand outdoor conditions, and using the wrong equipment outside can cause permanent damage, shortening the lifespan of your equipment. It's important to invest in quality outdoor speakers that are specifically designed to withstand outdoor conditions to ensure the longevity and optimal sound quality of your outdoor pool sound system.

Q: What should you avoid when researching pool or backyard sound systems?


A: When researching pool sound systems, it's essential to avoid low-quality brands, mix-and-match equipment, and amateur wire runs. You need to ensure that the wire is burial rated and the proper gauge for long runs. Using inadequate wires can lead to jacket degradation while using wires with a gauge that is too small can create zone sound issues as the sound travels and gets quieter. Additionally, it's crucial not to overlook the importance of integrating your pool sound system with the rest of your home's AV system to ensure seamless control and protect your investment.

Q: What is the most important consideration when researching pool sound systems?


A: The most important consideration when researching pool sound systems is knowing what kind of experience you want to create. Do you want to create a party scene or a relaxing atmosphere? Are you incorporating a TV or projector for outdoor movies? Understanding what you want will help you determine the right design and equipment for your pool or outdoor sound system and ensure that it meets your unique needs and expectations.

Q: What gets in the way of creating a great outdoor or pool sound system?


A: Several factors can get in the way of creating a great outdoor pool sound system, such as the landscaping, hardscaping, and the layout of your backyard. Misplaced speakers, trees, and sidewalks can interfere with wire runs and the even distribution of sound, while non-standard backyards with coves and asymmetrical layouts can present additional challenges.

Q: What makes a pool or backyard sound system easy to control?


A: A outdoor sound system's ease of control comes down to having a simple and intuitive interface that integrates your outdoor control systems and your indoor home sound system. Having a well-designed app or controller can make managing your pool sound and your indoor sound system a breeze.

Q: What should you look out for if you attempt to DIY your system?


A: If you attempt to DIY your pool sound system, you should look out for low-quality brands, amateur wire runs, speaker placement, and the quality of speakers that can withstand Florida's harsh environment exposure. It is best to consult with professionals in designing and installing your pool sound system to ensure optimal sound quality and longevity. You get what you pay for.
