Licensed Dealer

Cambridge Sound Management — Speech Privacy Done Right

Entertainment Pros is a licensed dealer of Cambridge Sound Management (now part of Biamp), the industry leader in sound masking and speech privacy systems. Their QtPro, Qt X, and Dynasound systems are trusted by hospitals, law firms, banks, and Fortune 500 companies worldwide.

Sound masking adds a barely perceptible background sound — engineered to match the frequency of human speech — so conversations one or two offices away become unintelligible. The result: HIPAA compliance for medical practices, attorney-client privilege protection for legal teams, and a calmer, more productive workspace for everyone.

  • Medical offices (HIPAA)
  • Law firms & courts
  • Banks & financial services
  • Executive suites
  • Open-plan offices
  • Therapy & counseling
