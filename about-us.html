About Entertainment Pros

A licensed, insured AV integration company serving Pinellas County since 2006. We deliver value-driven solutions for audio, video, security, and networking.

Our Story

Established 2006 in Palm Harbor, Florida

Entertainment Pros was founded with a clear mission: deliver value-driven solutions for audio, video, security, and networking to homeowners and businesses across the Tampa Bay area.

We are a residential audio/video integration, automation, control, and lighting company. From single-room setups to whole-house systems, every project receives the same level of care and precision.

We service all of Pinellas County, and areas of West Hillsborough and West Pasco. No matter the size of the project, we are focused on one thing — customer service.

Our team brings deep technical expertise to every installation, ensuring clean wiring, reliable performance, and systems that are easy for you to use every day.

Our Founder

Chip Tollefson

Chip Tollefson founded Entertainment Pros after serving as a Marine Corps avionics specialist from 1989 to 2005. His military background in precision electronics and complex systems translates directly to the meticulous installation work that defines every Entertainment Pros project.

That same discipline, attention to detail, and commitment to doing things right the first time is the foundation of our company culture. Every wire is routed with purpose, every component is calibrated for optimal performance.
