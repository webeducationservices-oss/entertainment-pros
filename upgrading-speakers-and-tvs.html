Upgrading Speakers and TVs: Time to Refresh Your Setup

Christina Tollefson October 29, 2025 3 min read

Home entertainment systems deteriorate over time. Picture brightness fades as backlights age in TVs older than five years, while speakers lose bass and treble response. At Entertainment Pros, we help clients determine whether a tune-up or complete upgrade makes sense — based on how the system sounds today, what it's missing, and what's possible with current technology.

How Sound and Video Have Evolved Over the Past 10 Years

Smarter Audio Everywhere

Modern systems minimize wiring and integrate seamlessly into home networks. Smart systems enable music streaming between rooms, synchronization with lighting and climate controls, and simplified control via smartphones.

Networking and Connectivity

Faster Wi-Fi and mesh networking allow components to communicate without disruptions. Modern components and TVs now connect directly to your home network, allowing seamless streaming without drops or lag.

Home Automation Integration

Audio systems now trigger automation routines. A single "Movie Night" command can close blinds, dim lights, and configure optimal AV settings through platforms like URC and Elan/Nice.

Prewired or Retrofit — You Have Options

Decade-old prewiring may be outdated, but modern solutions retrofit existing lines or operate wirelessly, modernizing homes without structural changes.

Multi-Room Audio

Distributed audio systems enable synchronized playback across multiple rooms and outdoor spaces, allowing music to follow you throughout the home.

Your Options for Upgrading: It Starts with a Site Survey

Upgrades should begin by identifying personal entertainment goals rather than purchasing standardized bundles. Every home has unique acoustics, wiring, and layouts. A professional site survey assesses the current system and identifies where upgrades deliver maximum impact.

Custom Recommendations, Not Cookie-Cutter Packages

  • Multi-room audio control with distributed amplifiers
  • Outdoor sound systems with weather-resistant speakers
  • TV brightness and display technology upgrades
  • Re-calibration and re-wiring of existing systems

We recently saw a high-end system serviced by two different companies — and neither one had ever taken the time to adjust the bass and treble. It took us about two minutes.

When It's Time to Replace Your TV

Dimming Screens and Half-Life

LED TVs lose brightness gradually. By five years, most operate at 70–80% of original luminance. If your TV looks washed out, it's not your imagination.

Color Accuracy

Older displays lack the black levels and color precision of newer QLED, OLED, and Mini-LED technologies, resulting in dingy whites and grayish blacks.

Sound Isn't Just About Volume

Thinner modern TVs have weaker built-in speakers, making external solutions like soundbars or surround systems necessary for quality audio.

When It's Time to Replace Your Speakers

Physical Wear

Premium speakers degrade over time as rubber surrounds, foam components, and connections deteriorate, causing loss of bass and treble response.

Technology Leap

Contemporary speaker technology offers improved wireless fidelity, amplifier efficiency, and streaming quality that surpasses aging equipment.

Better Balance and Integration

Modern systems provide consistent, balanced sound across multiple rooms with simplified control and platform integration.

What to Expect When You Upgrade

  1. Consultation and Site Survey: Assessment of current equipment, sound quality, cabling, and room acoustics.
  2. Personalized Design: Custom upgrade plans tailored to your specific needs.
  3. Professional Installation: Complete setup including mounting, wiring, calibration, and device integration.
  4. Smart Home Integration: One-touch control and custom automation scenes.
  5. After-Install Support: Ongoing adjustments and future upgrade assistance.

The Benefits of a Modern System

  • Brighter, truer color and contrast with QLED, OLED, and Mini-LED technology
  • Crisper, fuller sound from properly calibrated speakers
  • Simplified control through smart integration and universal remotes
  • Improved energy efficiency with modern components
  • Long-term reliability through professional installation and calibration
