Landscape & Exterior Lighting

Custom landscape lighting creates a dramatic first impression while adding safety and security to your home.

Outdoor Lighting Experts

Landscape Lighting Creates a Dramatic First Impression

Your home deserves to look as stunning at night as it does during the day. Professional landscape lighting transforms your outdoor spaces, highlighting architectural features, garden paths, and landscaping with warm, inviting illumination that makes your property stand out in the neighborhood.

LED lights are a money-saving upgrade that pay for themselves over time. Our custom, high-quality fixtures and color-changing LEDs add a layer of security while personalizing your home to reflect your style. Whether you want soft pathway lighting, dramatic uplighting on palm trees, or vibrant color-changing accents for entertaining, we design and install systems tailored to your vision.

As a licensed low-voltage contractor (ES12001376), Entertainment Pros designs, installs, and programs landscape lighting systems across Palm Harbor, Clearwater, and all of Pinellas County. We use only commercial-grade fixtures and LED technology built to withstand Florida’s heat, humidity, and salt air.

Why Invest

Benefits of Professional Landscape Lighting

More than just aesthetics — professional outdoor lighting delivers real value to your home.

Curb Appeal

Transform your home’s exterior into a showpiece. Strategically placed lighting highlights architectural details, landscaping, and hardscaping for maximum visual impact.

Security

A well-lit property deters intruders and provides peace of mind. Illuminate dark corners, entry points, and pathways so you always feel safe at home.

Energy Efficiency

Modern LED landscape lighting uses a fraction of the energy of traditional halogen fixtures. Lower utility bills and longer-lasting bulbs mean real savings year after year.

Property Value

Quality landscape lighting is an investment that increases your home’s market value. Buyers notice a beautifully lit exterior — it sets the tone before they ever walk inside.

What We Install

Custom Lighting Solutions

From subtle path lighting to dramatic architectural accents, we offer a full range of exterior lighting options.

Path & Walkway Lighting

Safe, elegant illumination for driveways, walkways, and garden paths. Guide guests with warm, welcoming light.

Architectural Uplighting

Dramatic uplights that highlight your home’s facade, columns, and architectural details for stunning nighttime appeal.

Color-Changing LED Systems

Set the mood for any occasion with RGBW color-changing LEDs. Perfect for holidays, parties, or everyday elegance.

Landscape & Garden Accent

Spotlight palm trees, garden beds, water features, and focal points in your yard for a resort-style atmosphere.

Security & Motion Lighting

Integrated motion-activated lights for entry points, garages, and dark areas around your property.

Smart Controls & Timers

Control your landscape lighting from your phone. Schedule scenes, adjust brightness, and automate sunset-to-sunrise operation.
