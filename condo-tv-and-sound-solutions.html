Condo TV & Sound Solutions

Framed TVs, Sound Systems & a Theater Experience

Condo Living

Creating the Perfect Condo Retreat

Your condo is more than a place to sleep — it is your personal retreat. Whether you are watching the sunset from your balcony, hosting friends for game night, or unwinding after a long day, the right entertainment setup transforms your condo into a space that looks and sounds incredible.

Condominiums present unique challenges and opportunities for audio-video installations. Shared walls, HOA regulations, limited space, and the premium placed on aesthetics all require a thoughtful approach. At Entertainment Pros, we have extensive experience working in condos throughout Pinellas County — from luxury high-rises on Clearwater Beach to cozy units in Dunedin and Largo.

We specialize in solutions that deliver outstanding entertainment without compromising the clean, curated look that makes your condo feel like home. Every product we recommend and every installation we complete is designed to complement your interior, not compete with it.

TV Options

Television Solutions for Condos

From art-inspired frames to weather-resistant outdoor models, we offer TV options designed for the way you live in your condo.

Framed TVs That Double as Art

The Samsung The Frame is the ultimate condo TV. When it is on, you have a stunning 4K QLED display. When it is off, it transforms into a curated piece of artwork with a customizable bezel that matches your decor. Choose from hundreds of paintings, photographs, and digital art pieces from the Samsung Art Store. Guests genuinely cannot tell it is a television until you turn it on. We install The Frame flush to the wall with hidden wiring for a seamless gallery look.

Outdoor Patio TVs

Many Pinellas County condos feature covered patios and balconies — perfect for outdoor entertainment. Weather-resistant outdoor TVs are built to handle Florida's heat, humidity, rain, and intense sunlight. These displays feature anti-glare screens that stay bright even in direct sun, sealed enclosures that protect against moisture and insects, and operating temperature ranges designed for our subtropical climate. Enjoy the game, a movie night under the stars, or morning news with your coffee on the lanai.

Sleek, Discreet TVs

For condo owners who want the biggest screen with the smallest footprint, ultra-thin TVs mounted flush to the wall create a streamlined, modern look. Motorized mounts can tilt, swivel, or even retract the TV into a cabinet or ceiling when not in use. Recessed installations set the TV into the wall itself, creating a flush, built-in appearance. These solutions maximize your living space while delivering the cinematic experience you want.

Sound Options

Sound Solutions Designed for Condos

Great sound does not require visible speakers. Our condo sound solutions deliver rich audio while respecting your space and your neighbors.

Invisible Speakers

Invisible speakers are built directly into your walls or ceilings and covered with drywall and paint. Once finished, they disappear completely — there is no grille, no visible hardware, nothing to indicate a speaker is there. The sound seems to come from everywhere and nowhere at the same time. This is the ultimate solution for design-conscious condo owners who refuse to compromise on aesthetics. We use Stealth Acoustics and similar brands that deliver audiophile-grade sound from a completely hidden source.

Ceiling Speakers

In-ceiling speakers offer a flexible, elegant audio solution for condos. Flush-mounted with paintable grilles that match your ceiling, they fill the room with rich, even sound without taking up any wall or floor space. Ceiling speakers are excellent for background music throughout the condo, and when configured as surround channels, they create an immersive listening experience. We install Monitor Audio and SpeakerCraft in-ceiling models that deliver clear highs and solid midrange without visual clutter.

Sound Bars

For condos where simplicity is key, a quality soundbar delivers a dramatic improvement over built-in TV speakers in a compact, attractive package. Modern soundbars from Sonos and Samsung pack multiple speaker drivers, Dolby Atmos processing, and wireless subwoofers into a single unit that sits beneath your TV or mounts cleanly on the wall. They are ideal for smaller living rooms, bedrooms, and spaces where a full speaker system is not practical. Many soundbars also support multi-room audio, so you can extend music to other rooms.

Professional Installation

Installation & Choosing a Local Installer

Condo installations require specific expertise. Here is what a professional installer brings to the table.

Optimal Equipment Selection

A condo is not a house. Equipment that works great in a 2,000 square foot home may be completely wrong for a 1,200 square foot condo. We select equipment sized and spec'd for your actual space.

System Compatibility

TVs, soundbars, receivers, speakers, streaming devices, and smart home systems all need to communicate seamlessly. We verify compatibility before purchase to avoid integration headaches.

Professional Installation

Condo walls are often concrete block or metal stud, requiring specialized mounting hardware and techniques. Our installers know how to work with every wall type and conceal wiring in challenging construction.

Post-Installation Support

After your system is installed and calibrated, we provide training on how to operate everything. If you need adjustments, have questions, or want to expand your system later, we are here to help.

Hiring Guide

How to Choose the Right Condo Installer

Condo work requires a different skill set than residential home installations. Here is what to look for.

Ask specifically about condo installations, not just general residential work. Condos have concrete or block walls, limited access to wall cavities, shared-wall noise concerns, and building management rules that a general installer may not know how to navigate. Ask for photos of past condo projects and references from condo owners. Entertainment Pros has completed installations in condos throughout Clearwater Beach, Indian Rocks Beach, St. Petersburg, and across Pinellas County.
Any installer working in a Florida condominium should hold a valid Low Voltage License from the state of Florida. This license is required for running wire through walls and ceilings. Additionally, confirm that the company carries general liability insurance and workers compensation coverage. Many condo associations require proof of insurance before allowing contractors to work in the building. Entertainment Pros holds Florida License ES12001376 and carries full insurance.
A good installer will visit your condo before quoting a price. They should assess your room layout, wall construction, existing wiring, and discuss your goals and budget. Be cautious of companies that provide quotes over the phone without seeing your space. Every condo is different, and a proper on-site evaluation prevents surprises during installation. We offer free in-home consultations throughout Pinellas County.
Your installer should provide a detailed, written estimate that breaks down equipment costs, labor, mounting hardware, and any additional materials. There should be no hidden fees or surprise charges after the work is done. At Entertainment Pros, we provide itemized quotes and discuss every cost before we begin. If something changes during installation, we communicate immediately and get your approval before proceeding.
Your relationship with your installer should not end when they walk out the door. Technology updates, firmware changes, new streaming services, and equipment additions all require ongoing support. Choose a company that will be there six months or six years from now when you need help. We have been serving Pinellas County since 2006, and many of our clients call us for system upgrades, troubleshooting, and additions long after the initial installation.
