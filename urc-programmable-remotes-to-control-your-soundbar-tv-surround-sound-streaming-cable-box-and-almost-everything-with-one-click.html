URC Programmable Remotes to Control Your Soundbar, TV, Surround Sound, Streaming, Cable Box, and Almost Everything with One Click

October 17, 2023 | By Entertainment Pros

One Click, One Remote

Modern homes are packed with lots of audio/video devices from Roku's to Apple TV's - Smart TVs to 5.2 (or more) surround sound, and along with this technological boon comes a pressing challenge – a confusing clutter of remotes. Every device and its remote bring a different set of settings for an optimized performance. As such, recalling the right settings to set the mood for relaxation or catch your favorite game becomes an ordeal.

  • The Dilemma: Multiple audio and video gadgets in modern households.
  • The Result: A confusing clutter of remotes
  • The Challenge: Remembering optimal settings for each device. ﻿

The All-Too-Common TV Remote Scenario

Here's a situation many can relate to: You're seated comfortably, ready to delve into your favorite show. You reach for the TV remote, only to realize there are three remotes which look almost identical. Once you've figured that out and turned on the TV, there's the challenge of selecting the right HDMI input. Then, the surround sound system needs syncing. If you have a Roku, AppleTV, Firestick, or any other device, which is another layer to the process. By the time you've got everything in order, you might find that you've missed something.

Surely, there's a more streamlined approach to all this?

Enter the Universal Remote Control, Inc. Solution

  • Who They Are: URC, the leader in home entertainment controls for over two decades.
  • Their Answer: The MX990 remote is designed to consolidate multiple remotes into one.
  • The Promise: Set up your entire entertainment system with a single button.

This is where Universal Remote Control, commonly known as URC, steps in to solve the remote problem. With a rich experience spanning over two decades, URC understands the intricacies of home entertainment systems. Their renowned MX990 remote is designed to consolidate the remote controls and settings from all your devices into one elegant solution. Imagine the convenience of setting up your entire entertainment system with a single button press. That's the promise of the MX990.

Customized One-Click TV Remote Entertainment

  • Personal Touch: A dedicated page listing all your sources.
  • Tailored for You: Custom programming suited to your devices and home.
  • Simplicity: Just press your desired source and let the remote handle the settings.

Beyond mere consolidation, the MX990 brings the power of personalization to your fingertips. This remote boasts a dedicated page which lists all your sources, whether it's a Smart TV, Apple TV, Roku, Cable Box, or any other device. What's even better is that the MX990's programming is tailored to your unique array of devices and your specific home environment. Just press your desired source, and let the remote handle the rest.

The Value of Expert Programming for Your Universal Remote Control (URC)

However, the true essence of the MX990 isn't just in its physical design or features; it's in its programming. While the remote is a marvel, its true potential is unlocked when it's set up correctly. There have been instances where DIY attempts led to less-than-optimal setups, resulting in undeserved negative reviews.

This is where experts like Entertainment Pros come in. With our touch, the MX990 is transformed into a seamless control hub, with minimal clutter and intuitive operations. Each button press, known as a macro, is designed to ensure that the right devices turn on with the correct settings every time.

Key URC Features:

  • Macros: Each button press ensures the right device settings every time.
  • Clutter-Free: An intuitive experience for all users.

Experiencing the Magic of One-Click Control

Here’s the MX990 in action: You wake the remote with a simple nudge, making the motion sensor turn it on. Then, with a single press on your chosen source, watch as your entire system syncs. The TV powers on and jumps to the correct input, the surround sound turns on, selects the proper input for your desired sources and also selects the optimum surround sound mode.

It even jumps to the right device control page for further selections. With this level of efficiency, everyone in the household can navigate the entertainment system effortlessly, eliminating the need for the usual flurry of questions about which remote does what or what setting is each device supposed to be on.
