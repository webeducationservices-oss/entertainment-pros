Mount Options

Types of TV Mounts

We install every type of TV mount to suit your room layout and viewing preferences.

Fixed Wall Mount

The slimmest option. Your TV sits flush against the wall with a low profile. Best for dedicated viewing positions where the angle does not need to change.

Tilting Bracket

Allows vertical tilt adjustment. Ideal for TVs mounted higher on the wall, such as above a fireplace, where you need to angle the screen downward.

Articulating Arm

Full motion mounting that swivels, tilts, and extends from the wall. Perfect for corner installations or rooms where you watch from multiple seating areas.

Ceiling Mount

Suspends the TV from the ceiling. Used in commercial spaces, outdoor patios, and rooms where wall mounting is not possible or practical.
