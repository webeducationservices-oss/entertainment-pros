LED Accent Lighting

Transform any room from ordinary to extraordinary with professionally installed LED accent lighting. No exposed strips, just a beautiful glow that elevates your entire space.

Elevate Your Space

Your AV System Deserves the Right Atmosphere

You installed a great audio-video system, but the room still feels boring. The picture is sharp, the sound is incredible, but something is missing. That something is the atmosphere — and LED accent lighting is how you create it.

Take advantage of new lighting technology to completely transform the look and feel of any room. Whether it is a home theater, living room, kitchen, or outdoor space, custom LED accent lighting adds a dimension that makes the space feel finished and immersive.

Get a Lighting Consultation
Professional Results

What to Expect with Professional Design

When you hire Entertainment Pros for LED accent lighting, you get results that look like they came with the house — not from a weekend project.

No Exposed Strips, Just the Glow

You should see the light, not the light source. We carefully conceal every LED strip behind molding, cove lighting, and architectural features so all you see is a smooth, even glow.

Experienced Techs, Professional Results

Our technicians have installed LED accent lighting in hundreds of homes across Pinellas County. We know the products, the techniques, and the pitfalls. You get a clean, lasting installation every time.

Quality That Lasts

We use commercial-grade LED strips with proper power supplies and controllers. No flickering, no color inconsistencies, no peeling adhesive. Just reliable, beautiful accent lighting for years to come.

Skip the DIY Headache

Why Professional Installation Matters

We have all seen those big box store LED strip kits that promise easy installation. Peel, stick, done! Except it never works out that way. The strips peel off after a week. The colors are uneven. The connections are ugly. And suddenly your relaxing accent lighting project has turned into an entire weekend of frustration.

Professional LED accent lighting is different. We use commercial-grade products with proper mounting, sealed connections, and controllers that actually work with your home automation system. The result is clean, consistent, and built to last.

Here is how it should go: we install your LED accent lighting, you pour yourself a cold beer, sit back, and admire the glow. That is the Entertainment Pros experience — drinking beer while admiring quality installation instead of pulling your hair out over a DIY disaster.

The Professional Difference

  • Concealed mounting — no visible strips or hardware
  • Even color distribution with no hot spots or dead zones
  • Proper power supply sizing for reliable operation
  • Integration with lighting control and automation
  • Color-changing and tunable white options
Applications

Where LED Accent Lighting Shines

LED accent lighting works beautifully in almost any space. Here are some of our most popular applications.

Home Theaters

Bias lighting behind the screen and cove lighting around the ceiling for the ultimate movie experience.

Living Rooms

Under-cabinet lighting, toe kicks, and shelf lighting that adds depth and warmth to your main living space.

Entertainment Spaces

Game rooms, bars, and man caves with color-changing LED accents that set the mood for fun.

Outdoor Areas

Deck lighting, patio accents, and poolside glow for outdoor entertaining after dark.

Kitchens

Under-cabinet task lighting and above-cabinet accent lighting that makes your kitchen glow.

Architectural Features

Highlight tray ceilings, niches, columns, and other architectural details with subtle LED accent lighting.
