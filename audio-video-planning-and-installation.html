Your Options

Enhancing Your Home Audio-Video System

There are several levels of audio enhancement. The right choice depends on your room, your expectations, and how you like to enjoy your entertainment.

TV Audio Only

Built-in TV speakers have improved significantly. For casual viewers who primarily watch the news and basic programming, your TV speakers may be sufficient. No additional equipment needed.

Soundbar System

A quality soundbar is a major step up from TV speakers. Perfect for smaller rooms or those who want better dialogue clarity and fuller sound without the complexity of a full surround system.

Surround Sound

A true surround sound system with dedicated speakers, a receiver, and a subwoofer delivers an immersive experience. Sound comes from all around you — the way movies and music are meant to be heard.

Honest Service

Don't Get Oversold by Other Installers

Many AV companies push expensive equipment that you do not need. A system that costs twice as much does not always sound twice as good. The key is matching the right equipment to your room and your listening preferences.

We take the time to understand how you actually use your entertainment system. Do you watch movies with the family? Stream music throughout the house? Host game day parties? The answers determine what you need — and just as importantly, what you do not.

Our goal is to deliver a system that exceeds your expectations without exceeding your budget. We want you as a customer for life, and that starts with trust.

Our promise: We will never recommend equipment you do not need. Every dollar you spend goes toward making your system sound better in your specific space. We design systems for real people, not showroom demos.
