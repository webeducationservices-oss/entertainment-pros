Exterior Illumination

Go Beyond Landscape Lighting

The first thing guests see is the front of your home. Light it up at night with professional exterior illumination that makes a lasting impression.

Curb Appeal After Dark

Make Your Home Shine

The first thing your guests see is the front of your home — and at night, it should look just as impressive as it does during the day. Professional exterior illumination transforms your home's curb appeal after dark, highlighting architectural features, landscaping, and pathways with carefully placed lighting.

We go beyond basic landscape lighting. Our exterior illumination services combine high-quality fixtures, color-changing LED technology, and smart controls to create a dramatic, personalized look that you can change with the seasons or your mood.

Professional Installation

Landscape & Outdoor Lighting Installation

Our landscape and outdoor lighting installation goes far beyond sticking a few solar lights in the ground. We trench wires properly, install professional-grade fixtures, and position every light to make your house look great while keeping trees, shrubs, and landscaping safe.

Every installation is designed for longevity. We use weather-rated fixtures, properly sealed connections, and low-voltage wiring that is safe and efficient. The result is a professional lighting system that enhances your property for years to come.

  • Professional wire trenching with minimal landscape disturbance
  • Fixtures positioned to highlight architectural features and landscaping
  • Low-voltage LED systems for safety and energy efficiency
  • Weather-rated fixtures built for Florida's climate
  • Tree and shrub lighting that is safe for your plants

What We Light

  • Home facades and architectural details
  • Walkways, driveways, and entryways
  • Trees, palms, and garden features
  • Pool and patio areas
  • Outdoor kitchens and living spaces
Next-Level Outdoor Lighting

Landscape Lighting Meets Home Automation

When you combine professional landscape lighting with home automation, you get next-level outdoor lighting that works on its own. Lights turn on at sunset and off at sunrise. Pathway lights illuminate when you pull into the driveway. Color scenes change automatically for holidays on your calendar.

Integrating your exterior illumination with your smart home system means you can control everything from your phone, set schedules, and create scenes that work together with your indoor lighting control system. It is the ultimate in convenience and curb appeal.

  • Custom scenes for entertaining, security, and everyday use
