Automated Shades

Motorized shade design, installation, and smart home integration for homes across Pinellas County.

Motorized Window Treatments

Automated Shades | Design, Installation, and Training

Motorized shades and screens offer protection from the sun, provide an added layer of security, prevent uncomfortable glare on screens and furniture, and significantly reduce the load on your HVAC system. In Florida’s intense sun, automated shades are not a luxury — they’re a smart investment in comfort and energy savings.

Automated shades adjust themselves throughout the day for perfect lighting at any time. Set schedules so your shades lower during the hottest afternoon hours and raise in the evening to enjoy your view. With one tap on your phone or a simple voice command, you can control every shade in your home — individually or all at once.

Entertainment Pros designs, installs, and programs motorized shade systems that integrate seamlessly with your existing smart home setup. Whether you’re starting from scratch or adding shades to an existing home automation system, we’ll make sure everything works together perfectly.

Key Benefits

Why Choose Motorized Shades?

Automated shades deliver comfort, convenience, and real energy savings every day.

Sun Protection

Block harsh Florida sun to protect furniture, flooring, and artwork from UV damage and fading.

Privacy & Security

Automated shades close on schedule even when you’re away, giving the appearance that someone is home.

Glare Reduction

Eliminate screen glare on TVs, monitors, and tablets. Perfect for home theaters and media rooms.

HVAC Savings

Reduce the load on your air conditioning by blocking solar heat gain. Lower energy bills, especially in summer.

Smart Integration

Seamless Smart Home Integration

Your automated shades don’t just work on their own — they become part of a connected home ecosystem. Pair them with smart lighting, temperature control, and automated lighting for a home that thinks for you.

  • Automatically close shades when you leave the house
  • Adjust shades based on sun position to keep your home comfortable
  • Coordinate with smart lighting for the perfect ambiance
  • Integrate with climate control to reduce energy consumption
  • Control via voice, app, remote, or wall switch

Works With Your System

Compatible with Lutron, Control4, Savant, and other major home automation platforms.

One-Touch Scenes

Create “Movie Night” or “Good Morning” scenes that adjust shades, lights, and temperature together.

Set It & Forget It

Shades That Adjust Themselves

Program your shades once and enjoy perfect lighting every day without lifting a finger.

Time-Based Scheduling

Set daily, weekly, or seasonal schedules. Shades adjust automatically based on your routine.

Sun Tracking

Intelligent sensors track the sun’s position and adjust shades to block direct sunlight while preserving natural light.

Sunrise & Sunset

Wake up to natural light as shades rise with the sun. Close automatically at sunset for privacy.
